Delete Your Data – MovieMatch

If you have signed in to the MovieMatch app using Google Sign-In and would like to request the deletion of some or all of your data, follow the steps below:

How to Request Data Deletion:

  • Send an email to: alwin3palackal@gmail.com
  • Use the subject line: "Delete My MovieMatch Data"
  • Mention the data you want to delete (e.g., Favorites, Watch Later list, preferences)
  • Include the Google email you used to sign in

What Will Be Deleted:

  • Your selected data (Favorites, Watch Later list, etc.) from Firestore

Retention:

Data will be permanently deleted within 7 days of receiving your request.

If you have any questions, contact us at: alwin3palackal@gmail.com
