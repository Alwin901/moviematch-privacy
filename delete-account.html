Delete Your Account – MovieMatch

If you signed in to the MovieMatch app using Google Sign-In and would like your account and associated data deleted, please follow the steps below:

How to Request Account Deletion:

  • Send an email to: alwin3palackal@gmail.com
  • Use the subject line: "Delete My MovieMatch Account"
  • Include your Google email used to sign in to the app.

What will be deleted:

  • Your authentication account (Google Sign-In link)
  • Favorites and Watch Later lists stored in Firestore
  • Any preferences linked to your account

Retention:

Deletion will be completed within 7 days of receiving the request. No data is retained afterward.

If you have any questions, contact us at: alwin3palackal@gmail.com
